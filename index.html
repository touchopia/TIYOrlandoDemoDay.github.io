Welcome to Demo Day!

The Iron Yard teaches intensive, 12 week courses that mentor students into professional, junior-level programmers. The Orlando campus of The Iron Yard offers courses in Front End Engineering, Ruby on Rails Engineering, and Mobile Engineering.

Demo Day is the culmination of these 12 weeks of immersive training, as students present the capstone projects they took from ideas to fully deployed web applications over the course of 2.5 weeks. The Iron Yard is proud to present the projects listed below, which are the capstone projects for the 2nd Orlando Front End Engineering cohort, and the 1st Ruby on Rails cohort.
